# Research Background

Knowledge Work Watcher is intended to support research on how knowledge workers
move among search systems, generative AI tools, and cited or discovered
sources. The MVP focuses on reconstructing sequences of exposure, attention,
and downstream navigation rather than collecting page content.

## Brief Source Notes

- Information foraging theory motivates examining transitions among
  information sources and cues.
- Search interaction research motivates query, result exposure, and click
  sequence measures.
- Human-AI interaction research motivates recording tool exposure and source
  use while separating behavioral metadata from content.
- Privacy-by-design and data-minimization principles motivate consent-gated,
  allowlist-first, locally queued collection.

## Research Constraints

- Ambient capture requires stronger visibility and pause controls than
  task-bounded instrumentation.
- Behavioral metadata can still be sensitive and must be minimized.
- Optional text capture is deferred pending an explicit research need, privacy
  review, and `TODO(IRB)` resolution.
- Claims about approved procedures, populations, or retention are `TODO(IRB)`.

Detailed literature review and formal citations are intentionally deferred
until study design work begins.
